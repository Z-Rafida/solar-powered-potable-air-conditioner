Solar-Powered Portable Air Conditioning

Innovative Cooling Solutions for Hot Areas in Ghana

Meet the Innovator

A young entrepreneur from Sunyani West, Bono Region, has developed a
sustainable cooling solution to combat extreme heat in rural areas with
limited electricity access. Using locally sourced materials and renewable
energy, such as solar power and evaporative cooling, the innovation
provides an affordable, eco-friendly alternative to conventional air
conditioning. The solution aims to improve living conditions, food
preservation, and comfort in homes, schools, and healthcare facilities.
Gaining interest from local authorities and investors, the entrepreneur seeks
partnerships to scale the project and enhance its impact in underserved communities.

Our Solution

This project provides affordable and sustainable cooling solutions that improve health, boost productivity, and enhance daily operations.
