About Our Solution

Addressing Climate Challenges in Ghana

Our solar-powered portable air conditioning units are designed to combat the intense heat experienced across Ghana, particularly in rural communities with limited access to electricity.

Our Mission

We're committed to providing affordable and sustainable cooling options that improve health, productivity, and daily operations for both individuals and businesses.

Our Approach

  • Energy-efficient design focused on sustainability
  • Local manufacturing to boost the Ghanaian economy
  • Community involvement in development and implementation
  • 100% solar-powered for areas with limited grid access

Meet The Innovator

Our founder is a young entrepreneur from the Bono Region of Ghana who experienced firsthand the challenges of intense heat in rural communities with limited electricity access.
Driven by a passion for sustainable technology and community development, he set out to create affordable cooling solutions that harness Ghana's abundant solar resources.
He aims to scale this innovation, providing technical training to local youth while addressing a critical need across hot regions of Ghana.
