Innovator

Ayeiri Salifu Abdalla, a young entrepreneur from Sunyani West, Bono Region, has developed a sustainable cooling solution to combat extreme heat in rural areas with limited electricity access. Using locally sourced materials and renewable energy, such as solar power and evaporative cooling, the innovation provides an affordable, eco-friendly alternative to conventional air conditioning. The solution aims to improve living conditions, food preservation, and comfort in homes, schools, and healthcare facilities. Gaining interest from local authorities and investors, the entrepreneur seeks partnerships to scale the project and enhance its impact in underserved communities.
